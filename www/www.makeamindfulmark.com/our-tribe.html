Our Tribe

Make a Mindful Mark is about helping people find that playful creative spirit within themselves. Entering the flow of mindful play provides relief from stress and encourages resilience, using simple tools and practices.

These playful practices also enhance our ability to see not just the object, or the obstacle, and its obvious characteristics, but in the space surrounding, to begin to sense the infinite potential for alternatives and opportunities. 

We invite you to join us, Kate and Peter, and our invited guest artists and practitioners, on this playful, meaningful journey!
