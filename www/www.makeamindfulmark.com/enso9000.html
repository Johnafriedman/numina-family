About ENSO9000

Our ENSO9000™ continuous circle improvement certification assures our clients, students, patrons and others of our commitment to the essential importance of Mindful Play, whether in the home, the studio, at work, in politics, business, academia, or other domains. :-) 

Of course, we're being clever, and we don't really certify anything, but we do believe very deeply that being honest and authentic in our practice is fundamental to our work—and our play!
