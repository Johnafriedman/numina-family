August 2019 Exhibit in Washburn

Atmospheres: Cloudscapes and Calligraphies

A Make a Mindful Mark Exhibit — New and retrospective work by Peter Fraterdeus, including grand scale panoramic photographs, East Asian Shodo, western calligraphy, lettercarving and more!

Please join the artist at the exhibit opening reception, August 8th, 2019 5-7pm

There will be demonstrations of calligraphy and letter-carving at the gallery on subsequent Thursdays.

Any of the photos can be provided for your business, home, office or studio as canvas, metal or acrylic prints. Bring the brilliance of the beautiful sky to your spaces!

Please contact us for information.
